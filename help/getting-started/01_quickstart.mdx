# Quick Start Guide

This guide will help you get started with TetiAI and explore its basic features in just a few minutes.

## Creating Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [app.teti.ai](https://app.teti.ai) and click on "Sign Up". You can register using your email address or with Google.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    If you signed up with email, check your inbox for a verification link. Click the link to verify your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or Join an Organization">
    After verification, you'll be prompted to create a new organization or join an existing one if you've been invited.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the Dashboard">
    Once your organization is set up, you'll be directed to the TetiAI dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Starting a Conversation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Chat">
    From the dashboard, click on the "Chat" option in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Begin Chatting">
    Type your first message in the text box at the bottom of the screen and press Enter or click the send button.
    
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/first-message.png" alt="First Message" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try These Examples">
    Here are some examples to get you started:
    - "What can you help me with?"
    - "Tell me about the features of TetiAI"
    - "How do I create a topic?"
    - "Remind me to check my emails tomorrow morning"
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Creating a Topic

Topics help you organize your conversations by subject, project, or any category you choose.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a New Topic">
    Click on the "+" button next to "Topics" in the sidebar, or type a message and ask TetiAI to create a topic from it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Topic">
    Give your topic a meaningful name that reflects its content or purpose.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Messages">
    Start chatting within your topic. All messages will be grouped under this topic for easy reference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connecting Your First App

TetiAI becomes more powerful when connected to your apps and services.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Apps">
    Click on "Apps" in the sidebar to access the App Marketplace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Available Apps">
    Browse through the available apps or search for a specific app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect an App">
    Click on an app you want to connect, then click the "Connect" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Follow the authentication flow to authorize TetiAI to access the selected app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Using the App">
    Return to chat and start using commands related to your connected app.
    
    Example: If you connected Gmail, try typing "Show me my recent emails" or "Draft an email to [contact]".
  </Step>
</Steps>
